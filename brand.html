New Institute of Creativity, Humanity & Entrepreneurship

A living mark. The N stays. The connector changes — a crayon line, a clasped hand, a flash of lightning — depending on which part of NICHE is speaking.

01 — The three marks

One N. Three meanings.

The vertical bars stay constant — the institute. The diagonal connector reflects the value being expressed.

C — for Creativity

The crayon line

An expressive, hand-drawn squiggle — playful, generative, unafraid to wander. Used in studios, the arts & design programs, exhibitions, student showcases.

H — for Humanity

The clasp

Two arms meeting at the centre — a handshake forming. Used for partnerships, community, ethics, the humanities, service programs.

E — for Entrepreneurship

The bolt

A diagonal lightning stroke — energy, ignition, momentum. Used for business, ventures, innovation labs, startups, demo days.

02 — A living identity

The diagonal moves with the moment.

For applications that represent the whole institute, the connector can rotate or animate across the three values. Below: a 9-second cycle.

Creativity. Humanity. Entrepreneurship.

The same N, animated through its three meanings — for digital headers, loading states, and motion identity.

03 — Wordmark & lockups

The full mark.

Custom-drawn letterforms built from the same geometric system as the N. Stroke weight, corner radius, and proportions are shared across the whole alphabet.

04 — Color palette

Cream, ink, gold — with three value accents.

Warm neutrals as the base. Each value owns one accent color, used when a program or campaign needs to identify itself.

Usage rule: the mark itself is monochrome — always rendered in one color, never multi-color. The value accents define the surface the mark sits on, the typography color, or the connector color, not all three at once.
