01 — The I

One dot, four residents.

In formal contexts the i goes undotted — the institution standing straight. In living contexts the dot is whoever occupies it. Click a resident to set the page's state.

03 — Rules

How the I behaves.

The other four never move

N, C, H, E are always Black 900 (cursor weight on screens is the only exception). All life is concentrated in the I — contrast is the point.

Formal = bar, living = shape

If the document outlives the moment (diploma, contract), the I is a black bar. If it speaks for now (poster, post, profile), it takes a resident.

One I per piece

The I never appears in two shapes on one surface. The slot has one occupant at a time — like a chair.
