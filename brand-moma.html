05 — Grid

Twelve columns, one rule.

Layouts sit on a 12-column grid. Sections divide with a single 1-pixel black rule — no boxes, no shadows, no rounded corners.

Type aligns left. Space does the rest.
Principle

Headlines span eight columns or more. Body copy never exceeds six. Captions sit flush to rules. Nothing is centered except by accident of symmetry.

Margins

40 px desktop, 20 px mobile. Vertical rhythm in multiples of 8.

Rules

1 px, full black, full width. Used to divide — never to decorate.

07 — Never

Three things we don't do.

Never decorate

No symbols, mascots, gradients, drop shadows, or rounded boxes. If a layout feels empty, the type isn't big enough.

Never mix colors

One program color per piece, flooded — or none at all. Two accents on one surface is a sale flyer, not an institution.

Never soften

The wordmark is always Black 900. No light versions, no outlines, no letter-spacing "for elegance." Heavy is the brand.
