The poster wall principle: every piece is built like street flyposting — one loud thing per sheet, type cropped by the edge, stickers and stamps allowed. English leads, Thai answers.
Crop it

Type is allowed to bleed off every edge. If the whole word fits comfortably, set it bigger.

Two voices

Anton shouts the headlines; Libre Franklin handles everything that must be read. Thai display set in Noto Sans Thai Black.

Flood + black

Same three program colors as v0.2, but harder: flat flood, black borders, no gray anywhere.
